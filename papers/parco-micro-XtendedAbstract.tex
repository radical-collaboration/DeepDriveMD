Stream processing capabilities are crucial to analyze and
derive real-time insights on incoming data from experiments, simulations, and
Internet-of-Things (IoT) sensors~\cite{streaming2015}. Prominent examples are
synchrotron light source experiments, such as those at the National
Synchrotron Light Sources II (NSLS-II) or the X-Ray Free Electron Laser (XFEL)
light sources. Some experiments at these light sources are projected to
generate data at rates of 20\,GB/sec~\cite{nsls}. This data needs to be
processed in a time-sensitive if not real-time manner, to support steering of
the experiments and computations~\cite{lcls_data}.
